History of the Game

First released on March 22, 1999
The first expansion pack, Added Attractions(1999)
The second expansion pack, Loopy Landscapes(2000)

:: Re-releases ::
Totally RollerCoaster(2001) including the base game and the Loopy Landscapes on separate discs
RollerCoaster Tycoon Gold(2002) bundling the base game and both expansion packs together
RollerCoaster Tycoon Deluxe(2003) that was later made available on digital distribution platforms


Evolution of RollerCoaster Tycoon (1999-2018)

How to Play

  • The main contents of the game are for the player, as a theme park entrepreneur, to successfully construct and maintain an amusement park.
  • Build various types of attractions, including roller coasters, carousels, bumper cars, haunted houses, Ferris wheels and so on. You can modify the park's geography and landscaping to better integrate the rides with the surrounding environment.
  • Strategically place food stalls, washrooms, information kiosks, etc., to meet the demands of visitors.
  • Hire mechanics, security guards, street sweeper, entertainers, etc.

Tips and Strategies

  • Create a small park in an area where its space is narrow for building rides and wide for constructing pathways.
  • Visitors can greatly enjoy the thrill of dropping from a high place if a tunnel is built in front of the descending point.
  • To reduce the number of visitors who feel nauseous, benches, fountains or trees should be strategically placed in front of the exit of rides like roller coasters.
